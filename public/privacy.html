Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Salvager ("we," "our," or "us") is a car salvage marketplace operated by Carsmart Inc. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Salvager mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use Salvager, we collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and ZIP code
  • Company name (optional, for dealers)
  • Vehicle listing details (make, model, year, photos, location)
  • Bid history and transaction records
  • Device push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the Salvager marketplace (listings, bids, account management)
  • To send SMS notifications about bids, listings, and account activity
  • To send push notifications through the Salvager app
  • To verify your phone number during account registration
  • To contact you regarding your account or transactions

3. SMS Communications

SMS messages are sent only to users who have explicitly opted in via the dedicated SMS consent checkbox during account signup, or by enabling SMS Notifications in the app's Profile screen. Providing a phone number for account verification does not by itself constitute consent to receive ongoing SMS notifications — consent is separate and optional.

If you opt in, you agree to receive SMS messages from Salvager (Carsmart Inc), including bid alerts, listing notifications, and account verification codes. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your activity. You may withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP to any Salvager message, or by toggling SMS Notifications off in the app. For help, reply HELP or email bids@salvager.bid. See the SMS Opt-In page for full details.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Salvager:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage
  • Twilio — SMS verification and notifications
  • Expo — mobile app platform and push notification delivery

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing their use of data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Vehicle listings are soft-deleted (marked inactive) and retained for record-keeping. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us at bids@salvager.bid.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Salvager is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Salvager after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
